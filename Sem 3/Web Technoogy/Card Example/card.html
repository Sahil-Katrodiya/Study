Gta 5

Grand Theft Auto V (GTA 5) is an action-adventure game where players control three protagonists: Michael De Santa, Franklin Clinton, and Trevor Philips, in the fictional city of Los Santos, based on Los Angeles. These characters, entangled in a criminal underworld, must pull off heists to survive in a ruthless environment, navigating both the criminal and government sectors.

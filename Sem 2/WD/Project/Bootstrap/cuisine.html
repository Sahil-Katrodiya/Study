North Indian Cuisine

North india is known for its rich,aromatic,and flavorful dishes often cooked with ghee and spices:


Aromatic and flavorful Biryani
  • Biryani: A fragrant rice dish made with aromatic spices and meat or vegetables.
  • Butter Chicken: A creamy,tomato-based curry that's a favorite worldwide.
  • Paneer Tikka: Marinated paneer cubes griled to perfaction.

Sweet Delights

No Indian meal is complete without indulging in some mouth watring desserts:


Irresistible Gulab Jamun
  • Gulab Jamun: Fried milk solids soked in sugar syrup.
  • Rasgulla: Soft and spongy chees balls in light sugar syrup.
  • Payasum: A South Indian dessert made of milk,rice, and jaggery.
